About SheetOps

SheetOps is a lightweight internal utility built to simplify Smartsheet administrative operations.

It enables users to securely connect using an API key and extract admin-level data in structured formats like CSV — without writing scripts or dealing with complex setups.

The focus is on speed, clarity, and controlled access based on usage tiers.

We offers flexible pricing tiers designed to grow with your needs — Free, Pro, and Enterprise.

  • The Free tier is perfect for getting started, allowing users to explore key features with access to up to 50 rows per feature.
  • The Pro tier unlocks complete organization-level data, enabling admins to gain full visibility across their Smartsheet environment without row limitations. For teams that need deep insights and complete control
  • The Enterprise tier provides unrestricted access to detailed data from individual sheets, reports, dashboards, and more — making it the most powerful option for advanced governance and large-scale operations.
